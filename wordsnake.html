🐍 WordSnake

Letters are scattered across the board. Steer your snake to collect them in order to spell a word — the letters join your current word at the top as you go.

Whenever your current word is a real word, it glows green and a Bank Word button appears. Bank it to score and reset your snake — or keep going to try for something longer.

Picked up a letter you didn't want? Steer into the eraser cube ⌫ to remove the most recent letter and carry on.

You get 5 snake resets — for when you tangle yourself up (crashing into a wall or your own tail uses one automatically) — and 5 board resets to reshuffle the letters and eraser whenever you like.

Desktop: arrow keys / WASD to steer, Enter to bank a valid word, Space to pause. Mobile: use the on-screen D-pad and the Bank word button.
