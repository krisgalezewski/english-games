⬇ WordDrop

A word teleports from its sentence to the top of the centre column and falls. Steer it into the correct part of speech column before it lands.

On mobile: swipe left/right to move · swipe down to drop · double-tap to pause/resume. On desktop: arrow keys to move · ↓ or Space to drop · Escape to pause.

You have 3 lives ❤️. Each wrong category costs a life. Reach rounds 6, 11 and 16 to unlock a new category and earn a bonus life.

New categories unlock every 5 rounds. Speed increases as you progress.

Starting categories

20 rounds · Up to 5 categories · 3 lives
