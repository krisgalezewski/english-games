⬇ WordDrop

A word detaches from its sentence and falls. Steer it into the correct part of speech column before it lands.

Use the ← → buttons or swipe left/right to move. Tap the column label to jump directly. ↓ or tap to drop. Space / double-tap to pause.

New categories unlock every 5 rounds. Speed increases as you progress.
